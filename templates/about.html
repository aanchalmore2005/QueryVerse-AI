About Our QueryVerse AI

A project by 2nd Year Computer Engineering students to simplify college information access.

QueryVerse AI is an intelligent virtual assistant designed to simplify how students explore and understand college-related information. Whether you're a new applicant or a current student, QueryVerse AI provides instant answers to common questions about courses, events, admissions, campus life, and more — anytime, anywhere.

With a focus on accessibility and ease of use, our platform ensures that users get the information they need without the hassle of navigating complex websites or waiting in long queues.

QueryVerse AI is created by passionate second-year Computer Engineering students who understand the challenges students face when trying to find reliable information. This project is more than just a chatbot — it’s our way of making campus life easier, smarter, and more connected for everyone.

Key Features

Explore the functionalities that make our QueryVerse AI a valuable tool:

  • Intelligent Chat Interface: A seamless and intuitive chat experience.
  • Comprehensive Knowledge Base: Utilizing data.json for quick answers.
  • AI-Powered New Question Handling: Leveraging Together.in for novel queries.
  • Speech-to-Text Functionality: Ask questions using your voice.
  • Emoji Support: Enhancing chat interaction.
  • Dark Mode: Comfortable viewing in different environments.
  • Chat History: Review your past conversations.
Backend: Python
Data Handling: Data.json
AI Integration: TogetherAi
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
